Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

3. Data Protection and Storage

We implement appropriate security measures (SSL, encryption, access control). Data may be stored on cloud servers located in India or outside, in compliance with applicable laws.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell your data.

  • We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze site usage. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. User Rights

  • Right to access, update or delete your data
  • Right to withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • For Indian users: Your data rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
  • For global users: Rights under GDPR, CCPA (if applicable)

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13. If we become aware, we will delete such information promptly.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with updated date stamps.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your rights, email us at privacy@corafit.com
